Feishu Real Write Adapter Phase 1 Runbook

Purpose:
1. Generate local Feishu write plans and payloads.
2. Keep dry-run enabled by default.
3. Review feishu_write_plan.json and feishu_write_payload.json before any real write.

Safety:
- Do not print or copy app secret, app token, table id, tenant token, cookie, or password.
- Do not write real Feishu unless both explicit environment switches are enabled and dry_run=false.
- Do not open Xiaohongshu.
- Do not call Yingdao.
- Do not open or close KuaJingVS shops.
- Do not upload MinIO objects.
- Do not write PostgreSQL.
- Do not trigger real n8n or OpenClaw.

Search dry-run:
.\scripts\xhs_feishu_plan_search_write.ps1 -BaseUrl "http://127.0.0.1:8000" -JobId "search-feishu-001" -AccountId "xhs_dev_01" -DryRun

Publish dry-run:
.\scripts\xhs_feishu_plan_publish_write.ps1 -BaseUrl "http://127.0.0.1:8000" -JobId "publish-feishu-001" -AccountId "xhs_dev_01" -DryRun

Real write gate:
1. XHS_FEISHU_WRITE_ENABLED=true
2. XHS_ALLOW_REAL_FEISHU_WRITE=true
3. Request dry_run=false
4. Feishu app id, app secret, app token, and target table id are configured
5. Payload scan passes
6. Operation is create or update
7. Update includes feishu_record_id
