XHS Local Full E2E Replay Runbook

Purpose:
- Run one local-only checkpoint chain across readiness, strict binding, hardened discovery, contract replay, and mock persistence replay.
- All outputs are local JSON files under .local_rpa_queue/e2e/{run_id}/.

Safety boundary:
- Do not write real Feishu.
- Do not connect real PostgreSQL.
- Do not upload real MinIO.
- Do not call real n8n.
- Do not call real OpenClaw.
- Do not call Yingdao OpenAPI.
- Do not open shop.
- Do not close shop.
- Do not open Xiaohongshu.
- Do not open external webpages.
- Do not search.
- Do not publish.
- Do not click real publish.

Order:
1. Make sure local hardened discovery exists.
2. Make sure strict account binding can pass for the account_id.
3. Run xhs_e2e_replay_search.ps1 or xhs_e2e_replay_publish.ps1.
4. For both paths, run xhs_e2e_replay_all.ps1.
5. Inspect e2e_input.json, e2e_result.json, e2e_summary.json, and e2e_artifacts_manifest.json.

Expected result:
- status is success when every local checkpoint passes.
- status is failed when readiness, strict binding, hardened discovery, contract replay, persistence replay, or sensitive scan fails.
- Failures still write local E2E result files for diagnosis.
